Product Management

SAP

What I did

Working on the product management team with one other co-op, I coordinated with members from the Product Management and Engineering teams to create field readiness materials for updated enterprise software products, including SAP HANA Studio, SAP Dyamic Tiering and SAP Event Stream Processing.

By improving the time it takes consultants, product managers and early adopters to become acquainted with SAP's new software and quarterly package updates, B2B deals and POCs for potential customers can be executed more effectively. This involved creating working demos and tutorials, including an IoT Starter Kit showcasing dynamic data analysis using Sybase CCL and SQL, and an information modeling module using SQLScript for SAP Dynamic Tiering.

What I loved about the job was its variety of tasks. On top of the technical writing, I programmed various C++ and Java input adapter modules for the various demos in remote Linux environments, and conducted in-person and online presentations to members of the local Product Management team, and consultants from around the world. Outside of work I joined the social committee to help plan many co-op and staff holiday events, including an office-wide Halloween candy trail.

Favourite Moment

Near the end of the term, I participated in the annual office-wide family feud competition with fellow product management colleagues. It was a lot of fun, and we totally killed it! Team PM ended up winning second place.
