About Me

Here are some things I feel very passionate about.

Tinkerer

I enjoy working with my hands to create functional things. I was the type of kid who built his own toys, and then taught others how to do so as well. This type of handiwork also extends into what I like to call "micro-engineering", which is solving small life problems with various mechanisms and gadgets. If something is broken, it delights me to be able to fix it.

Philosophy

Called "φιλοσοφία", or "the love for wisdom". I believe having a solid philosophy on life is important, for it trains the way one thinks, and the way one acts. A coherent method of thinking helps get rid of cognitive biases, and aids clear communication and extensive problem solving abilities. To fuel this process, I am an avid lifelong learner, with a strong motivation to gain experience on new topics. I am more than happy to chat about major world issues, the universe, free-will and beyond! Through this journey, I have found a love for educational YouTube videos, and scientific articles online. Feel free to check out my favourite blog, Wait But Why.
