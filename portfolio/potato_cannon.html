Potato Cannon

I designed and built this potato cannon during a high school summer, due to my love for backyard engineering projects. It is a combustion cannon made out of PVC piping, and is capable of firing a potato over 300 meters.

Story

To construct the cannon, I combined multiple existing online designs with my own creations including a handle, trigger, and tripod. A thick layer of duct tape around the chamber provided extra safety. To decide on the dimensions, I calculated optimal chamber/barrel volumes using ideal gas laws and air-fuel ratios based on the chemical properties of butane (my fuel of choice). This resulted in a much farther projectile range compared to cannons I saw online of similar size.
